Raider.io Score for La Metric Time

The app WoW Mythic Plus Score (raider.io) for La Metric Time is a tiny app, created with the La Metric Time development tools, to fetch your world of warcraft character details by the raider.io api.

The current update frequency is set to 10 minutes.

Your Details

  • Region: Choose between EU, US, KR, CN, TW.
  • Realm: Enter your realm name.
  • Character: Enter your character name.
  • Display Character Name: Decide wether you want your charactername be displayed.

In case you wish to show more than 1 character, simply duplicate the app within your La Metric mobile app and the details you need.

The script fetches always the score of the most recent mythic plus season.

How your data is processed

The app sends your details per webrequest to a little script, which is written in php. The script processes the request to the raider.io server and fetches your score to send it right back to your La Metric Time device.
